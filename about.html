About Us

Toll-free : 1800 876 8920

Doggoshop is one of the leading Pet shop chain situated in all major cities of India. We provide fast and safe delivery of Dogs in every corner of listed cities. We are the home of all popular dog breeds in India. Our best trainers and vets take care of them to keep them healthy and happy to handover to their new owner.

While the pet industry is showing an upward trend, it is yet to establish a strong foothold in India. In such a scenario, there is a need for someone to rise above the rest and bring in an organized platform for Dog lovers. This is precisely the space in which dogspot functions. Today Doggoshop.com is a destination for people seeking premium content. The team is well networked to gather information and present it correctly to the Indian Dog world from almost all parts of the country. In the last three years, Doggoshop.com has taken a lead in bringing the community closer. Amongst other initiatives, the team supported events in a big way. Earlier, all dog shows and other industry events used to happen in silos. Doggoshop.com with its information-sharing platform enabled such events to gain exposure globally. Today, the website helps events to get more participants, footfalls and make premium content available to dog lovers free of cost
